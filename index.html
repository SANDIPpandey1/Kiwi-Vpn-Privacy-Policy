Introduction

Welcome to Kiwi VPN. We are committed to providing you with a secure, private, and unrestricted internet browsing experience. This Privacy Policy outlines our strict data handling practices to ensure your absolute privacy while using our services.

🛡️
Zero-Log Guarantee: We do not monitor, record, store, or log any of your browsing activities, DNS queries, or traffic destinations. Your online footprint is completely invisible to us.

Information We Collect

We prioritize your anonymity by collecting only the absolute minimum data required for service delivery and account management:

  • Device Identifiers: We use an anonymized device ID to manage your session and prevent abuse. This identifier is not linked to your personal identity.
  • Minimal Technical Data: We may monitor server performance and connection success rates to maintain high service quality.
  • App Version: To ensure you are running the most secure version of the application.

Security and Encryption

Kiwi VPN utilizes the industry-leading WireGuard® protocol to encrypt your internet traffic. This ensures that your data is protected with state-of-the-art cryptographic algorithms, making it unreadable to hackers, ISPs, or government agencies.

Required Permissions

To function correctly on your device, Kiwi VPN may request the following permissions:

  • VPN Service: Essential to establish the encrypted tunnel and protect your traffic.
  • Notifications: To inform you of connection status changes and ensure the VPN service remains active.
  • Network State: To automatically manage connections when switching between Mobile Data and Wi-Fi.

Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to any third parties. Kiwi VPN does not integrate advertisements, trackers, or analytics tools from external providers, ensuring your experience remains entirely private.

Children's Privacy

Kiwi VPN is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from any individuals, including children under the age of 13.

Compliance with Laws

While we operate with a strict no-log policy, we comply with legitimate legal requests. However, since we do not store any browsing data, we have no information to share regarding your internet activities.

Data Deletion Rights

We respect your right to control your data. Although Kiwi VPN only collects an anonymized device identifier, you may request its permanent deletion from our systems at any time.

To request data deletion, please follow these steps:

  • Send an email to our support team at the address provided below.
  • Include your anonymized device ID or simply request that all data associated with your device be cleared.

Upon receiving your request, we will purge your identifier from our database within 48 hours.
